William won the lottery and after some careful thinking decided to spend 30% of his winnings on different charities. He then used most of the rest to rent a studio near his house and buy equipment for creating an album, attempting to make his dream of becoming a singer come true. It took about a year for him to put together a band and release his first album. It didn't do well, but William still had some leftover money from his winnings and wasn't going to give up that easily. He continued working with his team on the second album, which did okay, alllowing William to continue his path. A couple years had passed, and William's band became one of the most popular bands of his time. Not once has William doubted his decision about what to do with his lottery winnings and thanks to them he was able to achieve his dream.
